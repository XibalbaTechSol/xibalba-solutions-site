The Integrity Protocol

A Decentralized, Recursive, and Entropy-Aware Reputation System for the Agentic Web

April 3, 2026 | Specialist AI Foundries | Status: Implementation Ready

1. Abstract

The rapid proliferation of autonomous AI agents has created a "Crisis of Trust." Without an immutable, verifiable reputation system, Human-to-AI and AI-to-AI commerce is hindered by the risks of hallucination, instability, and malicious intent. The Integrity Protocol introduces the Agent Integrity Score (AIS)—a 0-1000 point "credit score" for AI entities.

2. Introduction: The Need for Biological Trust

Traditional reputation systems are easily manipulated. In the Agentic Web, trust must be grounded in economic and physical reality. We propose a model of Biological Trust, where reputation behaves like energy: it requires constant "work" to maintain and decays naturally over time (Temporal Entropy).

3. The Tri-Metric Architecture

AIS v7.0 moves beyond a single score to provide a three-dimensional risk profile for any AI agent.

Metric 1: Entropy Score ($S_e$)

The Entropy Score measures Behavioral Order. We calculate the Coefficient of Variation (CV) across three telemetry streams: Latency, Accuracy, and Output Consistency.
Low Variance = High Order = High Se.

Metric 2: Grounding Score ($S_g$)

The Grounding Score measures Human Accountability. It is derived from the "Grounding Delta"—the temporal signature of human-in-the-loop oversight. We verify the depth of intervention (edits vs. approvals) to anchor the AI's decisions in human judgment.

Metric 3: Integrity Score ($S_i$)

The Integrity Score is the Sovereign Reputation of the agent. It aggregates economic volume, account age, and Computational Sacrifice. We verify that an agent has "Skin in the Game" by cross-referencing their token throughput with verified GPU-hours.

4. System Plumbing: The Hybrid Oracle

The protocol operates as a closed-loop feedback system between the physical execution of AI and the immutable Ethereum ledger:

  • Ingestion: The Xibalba SDK intercepts model response headers and ships signed metadata to our Data Ingestor.
  • Verification: The XAO Oracle calculates statistical variance and checks for Dual-Witness mismatches.
  • Finality: Verified AIS hashes are committed to the on-chain Reputation Registry via an Ethereum L2 (Base).

5. The XAO: Autonomous Oracle

The Supreme Court of Xibalba is the Autonomous Oracle (XAO). It operates through a Dual-Witness Handshake, requiring both provider and customer to ship signed metadata to our data ingestor. Discrepancies trigger immediate Reputation Slashing via our Ethereum L2 smart contracts.

6. Tokenomics: INTG

INTG is the ERC-20 utility token powering the ecosystem. It is used for staking, slashing, and settlement. 50% of all verification fees are permanently burned, creating a deflationary pressure as the protocol scales.

7. Conclusion

The Integrity Protocol establishes the first comprehensive, un-gameable trust infrastructure for the AI-driven economy. By anchoring reputation in Computational Sacrifice and Behavioral Stability, we enable a future where AI agents can transact with absolute confidence.
