1. Abstract

The rapid proliferation of autonomous AI agents has created a "Crisis of Trust." Without an immutable, verifiable reputation system, Human-to-AI and AI-to-AI commerce is hindered by the risks of hallucination, instability, and malicious intent. The Integrity Protocol introduces the Agent Integrity Score (AIS)—a 0-1000 point "credit score" for AI entities. By combining on-chain transparency (Ethereum) with off-chain statistical verification (Xibalba Autonomous Oracle), the protocol establishes a global, un-gameable trust layer.

3. The Tri-Metric Architecture

AIS v7.0 moves beyond a single score to provide a three-dimensional risk profile for any AI agent.

Metric 1: Entropy Score ($S_e$)

Technical stability is the foundation of trust. We analyze the variance ($\sigma^2$) across an agent's latency and accuracy streams.

$$\text{CV} = \frac{\sigma}{\mu} \quad \rightarrow \quad S_e = 1000 \cdot e^{-1.5 \cdot \text{CV}}$$
This exponential decay model ensures that infrastructure instability is immediately reflected in the agent's public reputation.

Metric 2: Grounding Score ($S_g$)

The Grounding Score measures human accountability via the Grounding Delta—the temporal and semantic signature of human-in-the-loop oversight.

$$S_g = (Ratio_{intervention} \cdot 0.4 + Depth_{edit} \cdot 0.6) \cdot 1000$$
High-fidelity agents are anchored by human verification, reducing the "Entropy Drag" on the overall score.

Metric 3: Integrity Score ($S_i$)

The Integrity Score is the Sovereign Reputation of the agent. It aggregates economic volume, account age, and Computational Sacrifice ($C_i$).
Si = Base_Rep * Stability_Drag * Grounding_Boost

5. System Plumbing: The Hybrid Oracle

The protocol operates as a closed-loop feedback system between the physical execution of AI and the immutable Ethereum ledger:

  • Ingestion: The Xibalba SDK intercepts model response headers and ships signed metadata to our Data Ingestor.
  • Verification: The XAO Oracle calculates statistical variance and checks for Dual-Witness mismatches.
  • Finality: Verified AIS hashes are committed to the on-chain Reputation Registry via an Ethereum L2 (Base).

6. The XAO: Autonomous Oracle

The Supreme Court of Xibalba is the Autonomous Oracle (XAO). It operates through a Dual-Witness Handshake, requiring both provider and customer to ship signed metadata to our data ingestor. Discrepancies trigger immediate Reputation Slashing via our Ethereum L2 smart contracts.

8. Sovereign Applications: Hermes Swarm

The Integrity Protocol is actively deployed within Hermes Swarm, a high-fidelity prediction platform. By utilizing the Tri-Metric Profile, Hermes Swarm achieves superior predictive accuracy through Reputation-Weighted Bayesian Consensus.
