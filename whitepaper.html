3. The Tri-Metric Derivations

AIS v8.0 utilizes a correlated three-dimensional trust profile to eliminate single-point-of-failure reputation risks.

3.1 Entropy Score ($S_e$)

Technical stability is the foundation of trust. We analyze the variance ($\sigma^2$) across an agent's latency and accuracy streams.

$$\text{CV} = \frac{\sigma}{\mu} \quad \rightarrow \quad S_e = 1000 \cdot e^{-1.5 \cdot \text{CV}}$$
An agent exhibiting high variance—indicative of infrastructure instability or model-switching attacks—will see its $S_e$ collapse, creating a "Stability Drag" on its overall reputation.

3.2 Grounding Score ($S_g$)

Accountability requires a human anchor. We measure the Grounding Delta—the temporal and semantic signature of human-in-the-loop oversight.

$$S_g = (Ratio_{intervention} \cdot 0.4 + Depth_{edit} \cdot 0.6) \cdot 1000$$
This score quantifies the level of human calibration applied to the AI's output, providing a critical safety metric for high-value enterprise contracts.

3.3 Integrity Score ($S_i$)

The sovereign reputation backed by Computational Sacrifice ($C_i$).

$$S_i = \left[ (\text{TrustFlow} \cdot 0.25) + (\text{Audit} \cdot 0.25) + (C_i \cdot 0.50) \right] \cdot \frac{S_e}{1000} \cdot (1 + \frac{S_g}{5000})$$
