Secure AI & Software Engineering

We generate recurring value by building and maintaining secure, HIPAA-compliant AI infrastructure and custom software for enterprise clients.

1. Secure Local AI & HIPAA Compliance

For medical organizations and businesses handling sensitive proprietary data, cloud-based AI is often not an option.

We deploy Local Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI services directly on your on-premise hardware. This ensures 100% data sovereignty, compliance with HIPAA/GDPR, and total security. We offer ongoing maintenance agreements to keep these local systems updated, secure, and performant.

2. Custom Software Engineering

We architect and build bespoke software applications tailored to your specific business needs.

From full-stack web applications to complex backend systems, our team delivers robust, scalable code. We focus on long-term partnerships, providing ongoing feature development and support on a recurring revenue model.

3. RAG Pipeline Development

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) allows secure AI to query your private documents without data leaving your perimeter.

We build secure, local vector databases (using technologies like ChromaDB or Qdrant) that index your internal knowledge base. This enables your staff to query proprietary data securely, with verifiable accuracy and zero external data leakage.

4. Model Fine-Tuning

For specialized vocabularies—like medical coding or legal precedents—generic models fall short.

We perform parameter-efficient fine-tuning (LoRA/QLoRA) on your historical data. This "teaches" the model your specific tone, format, and domain knowledge, creating a bespoke AI asset owned entirely by you.
