Data Sovereignty Policy

At Xibalba Solutions, we believe that privacy is not a feature—it is a fundamental right. Our infrastructure is designed to give you absolute control over your intelligence.

1. The Sanctum Protocol

Our "Sanctum Guard" architecture ensures that mission-critical data remains within your private perimeter. We prioritize local execution and air-gapped runtimes to eliminate third-party API vulnerabilities.

2. Cryptographic Integrity

All telemetry processed by the XAO Oracle is signed and encrypted. We utilize Zero-Knowledge Reputation Proofs to allow you to verify your integrity without exposing proprietary logs.

3. Transparency

We are committed to open-source transparency. Our protocol and SDKs are public on GitHub, allowing for independent security audits by the community.
