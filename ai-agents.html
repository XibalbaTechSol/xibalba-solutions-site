Learning Loops & Autonomous Skills

Xibalba Solutions implements custom **Hermes Learning Loops** that allow agents to evolve. Instead of static responses, our agents analyze their own execution history to refine their internal "skill library" autonomously.

Through **Recursive Feedback Pipelines**, we bridge the gap between initial instruction and perfect execution. When an agent encounters a novel edge case, it doesn't just fail—it documents, learns, and builds a permanent autonomous skill to handle it in the future.
