The Neural Node Library

At the heart of SanctumBrain is a specialized library of functional nodes, each designed for specific high-fidelity tasks:

  • Action Nodes: Direct execution of CLI tasks, terminal commands, and system-level operations.
  • Bridge Nodes: Secure messaging relays between sovereign subagents and external platforms (Slack, Discord, Matrix).
  • Config Nodes: Real-time synchronization of neural parameters, global settings, and environmental variables.
